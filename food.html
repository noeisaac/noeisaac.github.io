    • Chorizo con Huevo (Spicy Mexican Sasuage with Egg)

    Chorizo con huevo is a Mexican dish that can be cooked for either breakfast, lunch , or dinner. Its simplicity and versatility make it a go-to dish whenever you are in a hurry or simply when you don't feel like making something complex. The two main ingredients used are chorizo, which is a spicy Mexican sausage, and eggs.
